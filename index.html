About Me

I'm an aerospace engineering student (he/him), who is currently working to complete a Master of Science at the University of Maryland in College Park. After graduating in May 2021, I will be working full time in the Space Industry as a Guidance, Navigation, and Controls Engineer (emphasis on Controls).

I'm currently learning about...

  • Julia development, with emphasis in projects relating to astrodynamics, robot kinematics, and dynamics & controls. Julia is by far my favorite programming language I've used so far in my ~6 years as an engineering student. I'm really looking forward to completing more personal and professional projects with Julia, and the tools it enables.

  • Force control law implementation for robot manipulators (largely with C++, and Julia for algorithmic design and testing). There are many different applications for force control. The one I'm personally most excited for is "zero gravity"!

  • Poetry! Someone close to me recommended Night Skies with Exit Wounds by Ocean Vuong, as well as a couple of poems by Maggie Smith. These works, as well as a wonderful interview with Tracey K Smith, have introduced me to poetry for the first time in 10+ years. I've really enjoyed learning how to read, analyze, and find poems, and I'm looking forward to learning more in 2021!
