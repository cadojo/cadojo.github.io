Casey and I have been looking for online museum exhibits that are Covid-safe. 
There are a lot of interesting exhibits that are fully virtual! This page contains 
a short list of some promising online museum tours & displays. 

## [Arts and Culture by Google](https://artsandculture.google.com)

Arts and Culture by Google introduces new content, and highlights existing
online museum content. It also has an app that uses augmented-reality to enable interactions with the virtual exhibits!

## [American Writer's Museum](https://americanwritersmuseum.org/virtual-hub/) 
The American Writer's Museum has tons of online content which explores 
American writers throughout history. Their [Frederick Douglass Exhibit](https://americanwritersmuseum.org/frederick-douglass-agitator-virtual/) looks really interesting. They _also_ have content available at Google's Arts and Culture.

## [National Women's History Museum](https://www.womenshistory.org/womens-history/online-exhibits) 
The National Women's History Museum has an insane amount of cool exhibits online. 
The [NASA](https://www.womenshistory.org/exhibits/women-nasa) exhibit looks really cool, but so 
do so many others.
