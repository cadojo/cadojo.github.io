Online Museums!

Published on January 23rd, 2021.

Overview

  1. Arts and Culture by Google
  2. American Writer's Museum
  3. National Women's History Museum

Casey and I have been looking for online museum exhibits that are Covid-safe. There are a lot of interesting exhibits that are fully virtual! This page contains a short list of some promising online museum tours & displays.

Arts and Culture by Google

Arts and Culture by Google introduces new content, and highlights existing online museum content. It also has an app that uses augmented-reality to enable interactions with the virtual exhibits!

American Writer's Museum

The American Writer's Museum has tons of online content which explores American writers throughout history. Their Frederick Douglass Exhibit looks really interesting. They also have content available at Google's Arts and Culture.

National Women's History Museum

The National Women's History Museum has an insane amount of cool exhibits online. The NASA exhibit looks really cool, but so do so many others.
