Personal Writing

A collection of pieces of writing that I'm drafting before publishing on jcarpinelli.dev, and other free-to-publish blogging sites.

Overview

This repository includes personal writing I'll be working on throughout 2021. Pieces will likely be provided via Markdown files, and will be organized / categorized in subdirectories. The first piece of writing I've included is a poem that attempts to connect Maggie Smith's Good Bones with the insurrection at the Capital on January 6th. I'm hoping that further developing this repository will lead to personal and professional growth throughout the new year. I'm also hoping that exploring topics through writing will allow me to see current events with new perspective.
